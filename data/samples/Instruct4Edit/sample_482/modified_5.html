Restaurant Borken

Terrace with lake view

The restaurant of camping Borken am See has a spacious and beautiful terrace with lake view. Between 8.00 and 10.00 you can smell the freshly baked bread buns. Enjoy a cappuccino, an aperitif for lunch or dinner or celebrate your holiday in the Hohe Mark with a cocktail. The dishes are carefully prepared to spoil your taste buds. Let us surprise you!

Menu and take away

To our specialties belong the crispy German schnitzels with a variety of sauces, grill dishes like spareribs and an assortment Pizzas. You have the possibility to to take the dishes to your own accommodation.
 

Child friendly restaurant

There is special attention for our young guests. They can play in the indoor playground or at one of the play sets around the restaurant. Have a good time on the famous bouncer or just relax with the making of a beautiful colouring page. And off course the children can count on a little present at his or her children’s dish.

Nice summer evenings

You can enjoy long summer evenings on our terrace with parasols. The restaurant of campsite Borken is opened all day long during high season (except the kitchen is closed on Mondays and Tuesdays, something to drink is possible).
