Victoria Saanich Women's Softball Association

VSWSA Joins Facebook!

5/21/2020

 
Help grow our Victoria Saanich Women's Softball Association online presence by "liking" the new Facebook Page. Please invite your friends and family to like the page so everyone can stay up to date with the latest information!

Softball Canada Cancels 2020 Canadian Championships

5/20/2020

 
After carefully considering all factors at play, Softball Canada’s Board of Directors has cancelled all of its Canadian Fast Pitch and Slo-Pitch Championships for the 2020 season due to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic. Please visit softball.ca for more information.

Extension For No Softball Activities - June 1, 2020.

4/17/2020

 
On Thursday April 16, Softball BC announced that all softball acitivities are hereby delayed until June 1, 2020. At this point in time, provincial and regional championships remain in place for 2020. Please visit softball.bc.ca for more information.
