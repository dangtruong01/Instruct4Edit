The Carbon Conversation

31/03/2022
Shuttleworth College
Visit The Carbon Conversation

More information about The Carbon Conversation

Carbon is one of the hottest topics in agriculture at the moment. With so many questions surrounding carbon schemes, regenerative farming approaches, how carbon should be measured and bigger discussions determining what soil carbon actually is, hopefully, this series of events can help resolve issues and inspire intervention.

At The Carbon Conversation, we look forward to being joined by a range of speakers sharing their approaches and journey’s towards low-carbon agriculture, regenerative farming as well as the highs and lows of the current carbon schemes. This includes…

  • Thomas Gent – UK Country Manager and Head of Certificates for the Agreena Carbon Programme
  • Tim Field – Founder/Board Member Agricology, Facilitator of the North East Cotswold Farmer Cluster and CEO of Carbon Quester
  • Peter Allen – Farmer and Contractor on the Shuttleworth Estate
  • Phil Jarvis – Chairman of Farming and Environment at Albanwise Farming

An open discussion will be at the heart of the event to further raise and answer the questions surrounding sustainable farming practices. This will help shape our future events in The Shuttleworth Series.

The event takes place at 7 pm on 31 March at Shuttleworth College. It is open to all and is FREE to attend.
