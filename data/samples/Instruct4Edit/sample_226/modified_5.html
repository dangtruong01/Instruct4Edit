Project Coordinator: Jody Bailey

Subbasin: Lower Iowa Watershed (07080209)

The English River watershed, located in southeastern Iowa, is 409,236 acres (approximately 639 square miles) in area. It is home to approximately 21,700 people, the majority of whom live in one of several small communities in the watershed.
