Noise and Vibration Control

CARRINGTON ENGINEERING SALES has been the representative for Kinetics Noise Control in the Carolina’s and Virginia’s for over 15 years.  We have been involved with the supply and installation of noise reduction systems for industrial, military and large commercial installations throughout our region.  We are available to help determine the needs required and the products necessary to provide a more quiet and safe environment for your neighbors and for you employees.

KINETICS NOISE CONTROL celebrated their 50th year in 2008.  They have extensive experience in designing and manufacturing innovative products to control sound and vibration. Established in 1958 as engineers focusing on sound and vibration control, Kinetics pioneered development of pre-compressed, molded fiberglass pad isolators that would be incorporated into an innovative new floor isolation system. Previous trade names of Kinetics Noise Control include Consolidated Kinetics and Peabody Noise Control.

Over fifty years later, Kinetics’ now produces the industry’s largest selection of innovative products to address vibration and noise control, room acoustics, and seismic restraint concerns for almost any building application. Value is added with our experienced staff of engineering and customer support people ready to work with you.
