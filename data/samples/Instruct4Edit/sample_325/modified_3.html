The Cynon Valley in 2003

These extracts are taken from the current Ordnance Survey 1:50,000 (one and a quarter inch to one mile map) published on their web site. They should cover the areas where most of us lived. Click on the thumbnails to enlarge them. The enlarged maps are approximately double the scale of the printed original map, so each has an on-screen scale of about two and a half inches to one mile.
