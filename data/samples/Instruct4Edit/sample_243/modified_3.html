Summer Nights With the Northwoods Baseball League

Jul 15, 2017
Summer is in full swing at the Stewart Inn in Wausau, Wisconsin.  Our calendar is booking up quickly, but there's still plenty of time to get here and enjoy everything this beautiful region has to offer. Wausau is a quaint, charming, and adventurous city in Wisconsin. As a travel destination, it's truly a hidden gem, and is arguably one of the top outdoor destinations in the state. Outdoor enthusiasts from all across the country descend upon Wausau for a variety of reasons, whether it's for the whitewater on the Wisconsin River, or the beautiful woodland forests. Move from one adventure to another, and you'll soon see why we think Wausau is such a special town.  When you're looking for a little downtime between adventures, there's plenty of ways to relax, too.  One of our favorites is catching a summer baseball games with the Wisconsin Woodchucks  Book your room at the Stewart Inn Boutique Hotel, and come join us for America's favorite pastime!

Summer Baseball Games

Most of your day in Wausau should be spent exploring, whether in the downtown core, or out in the abundant parks and trails that surround the city.  That's just part of what you do here.  As much as we know you'd probably like to, it's impossible to be out there adventuring all of the time.  Eventually, you'll need to come back to the Stewart Inn and enjoy a relaxing evening or two around the town. You're in luck!  Wausau is an incredible destination, with delicious foodie eats, inspiring artistic museums and galleries, wonderful shopping, and plenty of entertainment in the streets of downtown. Wausau is also home to a popular Northwoods Baseball Team.  What better way to relax, than with a cold beer and America's favorite pastime? The Northwoods Baseball League is a summer collegiate league, and their season runs from now through the end of August. Our local team, the Wisconsin Woodchucks, have even been chosen to host the 2017 All-Star Game & Home-Run Derby, which happens next week at Wausau's historic Athletic Park.  Tickets aren't very expensive, and your enjoyment is more than worth the cost. After the game, it's time to sink into the luxurious accommodations at Stewart Inn's Boutique Hotel.  You'll enjoy the most restful night of sleep away from home.  Wausau is the place to be this summer. Book your room with us today!
