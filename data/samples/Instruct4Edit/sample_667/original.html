Australia’s Silverchain Group Visits Sheba

by admin | May 1, 2023 | Australia | 0 comments

Sheba medical Center and Sheba Beyond recently welcomed the innovative Silverchain Group, Australia’s leading care-at-home provider for 100+ years. Grateful for the presence of Dale Fisher, CEO, and Anna Barker, Executive Director for Research and Innovation. This exchange of insights and best practices in hospital-at-home has us buzzing with potential!
