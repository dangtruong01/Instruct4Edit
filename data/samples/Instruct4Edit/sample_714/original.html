Highlights of the Day…

  • 7th round of the Castle Combe Racing Club Championships… –  Castle Combe Saloon Car Championship –  All New Castle Combe GT Championship –  Formula Ford 1600s Championship
  • 2 x races for MINI Challenge Championship (modern BMW Mini’s)
  • 2 x races Radical SR3 Challenge
  • 2 x races Radical Clubman’s Cup

“Join us as the UK’s largest motorsport organiser, Motor Sport Vision Racing (MSVR), showcases its top races with the MINI Challenge Championship, Radical SR3 Challenge and the Radical Clubman’s Cup. With a bucket-load of downforce, the Radical’s speed and grip needs to be seen to be believed. With the Castle Combe Championship past the midway point, things could be getting very exciting indeed…”
