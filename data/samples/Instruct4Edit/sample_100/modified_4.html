8 Street Performances You’ll Love at Magic Kingdom

4) Dapper Dans

When old school fans of Disney World think of Magic Kingdom, the Dapper Dans certainly come to mind. This group of four men entertain guests on Main Street from park open until the middle of the afternoon and they are a treat. Dressed in candy stripe vests with matching hats, they sing their way into your heart and leave you wondering why you never saw them perform before!
