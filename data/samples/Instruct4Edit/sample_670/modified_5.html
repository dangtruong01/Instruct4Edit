Understand Mortgage Options

Securing a mortgage as a foreign buyer in the Bahamas is indeed possible, but it’s important to understand that the process may differ from what you’re accustomed to in your home country. Fortunately, The Bahamas boasts a variety of banking institutions that offer mortgage services tailored to international buyers, residents, and locals. These services encompass construction loans, home equity loans, and conventional mortgages for residential properties.

Investment Opportunities

The Bahamas offers a wide range of investment opportunities in real estate, tourism, and various other sectors. The government actively welcomes foreign investors and has relatively minimal restrictions on property ownership. One noteworthy incentive for investors is the potential to accelerate their Permanent Residency status application by purchasing homes valued at $1.5 million or more.
